How safe is your food

There is a wave of cyclosporiasis currently happening in the United States. Cyclosporiasis is caused by a parasite called Cyclospora. Symptoms of cyclosporiasis include watery diarrhea that relapses multiple times, nausea, fatigue, and stomach cramping among others. Cyclosporiasis is typically contracted when someone eats cyclospora oocysts in their food. This app will help you determine what the risk of contracting cyclospora from your food and how to minimize the likelihood of illness.
